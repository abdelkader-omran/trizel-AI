INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY NON-APPROPRIATION POLICY
=============================================

Project : TRIZEL Epistemic Operational Portal
Target  : https://trizel-ai.com/
Phase   : FINAL_EXECUTION (Phase-5)
Doc ID  : LEGAL-IP-NON-APPROPRIATION-v1.0
Status  : Governance-locked

----------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE
----------------------------------------------------------------
This policy establishes binding guarantees that TRIZEL does not
appropriate, absorb, or reassign intellectual property originating
from external authors or upstream repositories.

This document is legally and epistemically binding.

----------------------------------------------------------------
2. FUNDAMENTAL PRINCIPLE
----------------------------------------------------------------
All intellectual property remains with its original author(s)
or owning institution(s).

TRIZEL SHALL NOT, under any circumstance:
- claim ownership,
- imply ownership,
- transfer ownership,
- dilute authorship,
- or relicense third-party work.

----------------------------------------------------------------
3. SCOPE
----------------------------------------------------------------
This policy applies to:
- external researcher submissions,
- upstream producer repositories,
- upstream integrator repositories,
- datasets, theories, analyses, figures, and publications,
- all content referenced or exposed by the TRIZEL portal.

----------------------------------------------------------------
4. NON-APPROPRIATION GUARANTEES
----------------------------------------------------------------
TRIZEL guarantees the following:

- No ingestion of external work into TRIZEL ownership
- No rehosting of full external content without consent
- No modification of original intellectual material
- No derivative claim beyond explicit analytical reference
- No exclusive rights asserted by TRIZEL

----------------------------------------------------------------
5. CONDITIONS FOR REFERENCE
----------------------------------------------------------------
TRIZEL may reference external work ONLY if:
- authorship is explicit and visible,
- provenance is complete and traceable,
- consent for reference is documented,
- exposure is limited to metadata and links unless otherwise agreed.

Absence of consent prohibits exposure.

----------------------------------------------------------------
6. PROHIBITED PRACTICES
----------------------------------------------------------------
The following are strictly forbidden:

- copying external text, figures, or data into the portal
- summarizing or paraphrasing theories without consent
- presenting third-party work as TRIZEL output
- rebranding external work under TRIZEL identity
- obscuring original publication venues

Any violation blocks deployment.

----------------------------------------------------------------
7. AUTHOR RIGHTS PRESERVATION
----------------------------------------------------------------
Authors retain full rights to:
- publish independently,
- withdraw consent,
- request correction or removal of references,
- control licensing of their work.

TRIZEL must comply without delay.

----------------------------------------------------------------
8. WITHDRAWAL AND REMOVAL
----------------------------------------------------------------
Upon author request:
- references MUST be removed,
- links MUST be disabled,
- audit records MUST remain intact (metadata only).

No retaliation or penalty is permitted.

----------------------------------------------------------------
9. AUDIT AND TRANSPARENCY
----------------------------------------------------------------
All references to external intellectual property MUST be:
- logged,
- traceable,
- auditable.

Hidden or implicit appropriation is prohibited.

----------------------------------------------------------------
10. ENFORCEMENT
----------------------------------------------------------------
Violation of this policy results in:
- immediate deployment blockage,
- mandatory corrective action,
- potential governance sanctions.

----------------------------------------------------------------
11. VERSIONING AND AMENDMENT
----------------------------------------------------------------
This policy is version-locked.

Any amendment requires:
- explicit governance approval,
- version increment,
- recorded commit hash.

----------------------------------------------------------------
AUTHORITY
----------------------------------------------------------------
This policy is binding for Phase-5 FINAL_EXECUTION.
Non-compliance invalidates deployment eligibility.
